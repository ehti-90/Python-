#Higher Lower - Followers Edition

- A terminal-based guessing game where you compare social media follower counts and try to pick who has more.

##How to Play

You're shown two accounts (A and B) with their name, country, and description. Guess which one has more followers by typing A or B. Get it right and the game continues — your previous B becomes the new A. Get it wrong and the game ends with your final score.

##Notes

- Follower counts are in millions
